Despite the flourishing of massive online courses or MOOC started at the end of 2011, today at 2013 there are many course offerings that people could enroll in, but they don't because of language barriers. Quantum University Project is born to provide such content starting by spanish speaking people all over the world. Many universities from Spain has started to create online courses following the inspiration started by universities at United States. However, is that content of the same quality than their physical counterparts? Do students get the chance of develop their full potential?

Quantum is the name of a new startup whose goal is to create courses and content to provide high education at low cost. The primary language of the site is spanish, and the first course is an introduction to Mandarin Chinese. The aim of the campaign is to get support for our staff and pay for website development.

Our team believes we can improve the actual offering by providing high quality education combined with a platform that focus on practice in order to get an accelerated learning with real results. Sharing, getting feedback from other students and guidance from their instructors is also key to evolve the actual education.

FAQ:

What's Quantum University Project?
This is the internal codename that refers to the company organization, the course contents and the technology that makes it possible.
What's Universidad Quantum?
Quantum is the company supporting this project to deliver high quality education. In physics, a quantum is the minimum amount of any physical entity or energy to change its state. Hence, Universidad Quantum focus on providing the knowledge and the tools to empower people to create a change in his career or education path.

Quantum is in its startup phase. Launching the project to the world and getting funding through a crowdfunding campaing.
